# Task Specifications

## Task 1: Wealth Accumulation (Easy)
**Goal**: Achieve steady wealth growth

**Observable State**:
- Current portfolio value
- Income, savings, net worth
- Asset allocation

**Grading Criteria**:
- Net worth accumulation (60%)
- Savings maintenance (20%)
- Portfolio growth (20%)

---

## Task 2: Crisis Management (Medium)  
**Goal**: Preserve capital during market downturns

**Observable State**:
- All Task 1 + market volatility (VIX)
- Portfolio drawdown
- Market regime

**Grading Criteria**:
- Capital preservation (50%)
- Diversification (20%)
- Recovery speed (20%)
- Tax optimization (10%)

---

## Task 3: Portfolio Optimization (Hard)
**Goal**: Multi-objective optimization

**Observable State**: 
- Complete market & portfolio state
- Multiple competing objectives

**Grading Criteria**:
- Wealth accumulation (30%)
- Risk-adjusted returns (25%)
- Tax efficiency (20%)
- Goal achievement (15%)
- Diversification (10%)

---

## Scoring

All tasks scored on scale **0.0 → 1.0**:
- 0.0-0.3: Poor (goals not met)
- 0.3-0.6: Fair (some progress)
- 0.6-0.8: Good (most objectives met)
- 0.8-1.0: Excellent (optimized performance)
